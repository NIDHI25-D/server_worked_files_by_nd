Custom module for Sales Marvelsa
----------------------------------------
##### USAGE

* This module are use for multiple usages, the functionality of this module are as follow.
* there are the delivery section in website while creating an order, so if there are the free shipping so the message are displayed which is filled in the field - **Display Message On Website website**.   
* there are the Available Credit related message are shown under the checkout button at the website, as an information or warning as per the credit limit of the user and Is Credit ? is set in pricelist which is used in the order. 
* if the product are over sized there are the message shown after finalized the payment and for those product's there are Oversized field are checked if product are oversized, it is also reflected to the Picking and Invoices respective it's sale order.
* there are added custom fields in **sale report** i.e - **Ruta**, **Zone**, **Delivery locality**, **Delivery state** which is opened under the menu **Dasboard Logistics**
* there are the menu are created **Productos a recibir** in this menu there are stock move's list view form view and kanban view are available.

##### Configuration

* There are various configuration for different parts.
* there are various groups are available here like - **Create Delivery Addresses**, **Change discount column**, **Permission of Legal Person**
* **Create Delivery Addresses** are enable the menu **Delivery addresses** which is used for partner's delivery address.
* **Change discount column** are enable to edit the **Is Discount Group Applied** column in sale order line.
* **Permission of Legal Person** are enable the menu **Legal Person** under contacts, under configuration there are the menu Legal Person, where creates the legal person's records and those are reflected at the field Legal Person in the Partner. 
* in configuration(Settings) there are the field **Customer Agrobolder** and from this field there are the message(reason) are shown in website.

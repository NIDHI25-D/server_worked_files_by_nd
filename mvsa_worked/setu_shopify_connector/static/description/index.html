Shopify Odoo Connector

OVERVIEW

With this fastest growing digital transformation of business, use of E-Commerce cloud based platforms is must. Shopify is one such leading, powerful eCommerce platform in the market right now, which allows its users to build their own online stores with many built-in features.

To manage various functionalities that Shopify provides as a powerful e-Commerce platform, the need for the most effective ERP management System that operates all transactions providing a user friendly environment is a must. Odoo is the best and most famous Open source ERP management system providing excellent, all in one features - accounting, sales, inventory management, manufacturing, marketing, easiest app integration, most flexible for customization and interface.

Hence we have developed all-in-one Shopify Odoo connector - An Omni channel solution that integrates Odoo ERP and Shopify eCommerce to manage various functionalities of Shopify which aids not only to end users but also to the management team of Shopify. By using this connector various functionalities like importing products and its details, customer details, orders, stock details, reports,invoices, returns, refunds, point of Sale.

    Advantages

    Update order shipping status

    The status of shipped orders is auto updated from Odoo to Shopify

    Synchronize products

    Synchronize Import and export of products and its details between Shopify and Odoo

    Automated workflow

    Various operations like confirming order, creating and validating invoices, register payment can be operated in fully automated

    Financial status of Shopify orders

    Allows you to take necessary actions like confirming order, creating and validating invoices and many for all financial status of orders in Shopify directly from Odoo.

    Schedule base synchronization

    Seamlessly allows various processes of import and export to be operated in an automated way during schedule based time decided by the user.

    Real time synchronization

    Seamlessly allows various processes of import to be operated in an automated way at any instant.

    Process History

    Helps you to analyze the failures in any of the operations performed whether it is of import process or export process.

    Analyzing risky orders

    Details about failures of any orders and its payment in Shopify can be analyzed very easily directly from Odoo.

    Return and Refunds management

    Seamlessly manage details of Return and Refunds between Shopify and Odoo

    Stock management

    Seamlessly manage details of stock between Shopify and Odoo

    Price management

    Synchronize the details about changes in price of products between Shopify and Odoo.

Setu Customer Statistics On Portal
---------------

##### Overview

    The My Statistics page in the Odoo portal provides a comprehensive view of your
    purchasing trends, payments, and best-selling products in your area.

    This module presents data through interactive charts and tables, making 
    it easy to track your business performance.

##### Features
    
   ✔ Purchase History Chart – Compare your purchases for the current month,previous month and current month of last year.

   ✔ Category-Wise Purchase Trends – View purchase distribution by product categories which includes purchase of current month,previous month and Previous Quarter months.

   ✔ Pending Invoice Summary – See Invoice Paid After Due Date and Invoice Paid On Time.

   ✔ Best-Selling Products – Discover the top products in your area with direct links to buy.
                             This also include products with Types : Finished Products,Spare Part Products,Accessory Products

   ✔ Delivery Time Statistics – Analyze delivery timelines for your orders.

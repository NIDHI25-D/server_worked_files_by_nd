SGEEDE Minimum Price Module
-----------------------------

### USAGE

* Lock and control your minimum sale price to POS, Sales Order and Invoice.
* Have more control over your product pricing with SGEEDE minimum price module, prevent sale order and invoicing that is lower than the minimum price required and avoid extremely cut discount prices in the point of sale module.

### Informative warning for user  
* The module will give your employee informative warning of the product that is lower than the minimum price so they can recheck the price and minimum price of the product. Remove the worries of having to watch the sales constantly to prevent extremely cut prices.

### Control Over POS Discount Pricing
* Prevent steep discount pricing as this module will count over discounting and quantity to prevent discounted price below the minimum price to be sold.

EDI for Mexico Extended
----------------------------------------
##### USAGE

* This module are mainly use for CFDI related modification.
* here are adding custom things during the sign CFDI documents, i.e - write the value of the CFDI name(which we taken here in account.move) as an current attachment name and those attachment are made publicly, which are creating for EDI document.     
* here are added a fields **IGI** and **IVA** at Product Template.
* here are the automation of the fiscal position within the contacts and added the selection of the Fiscal Regime - **No Fiscal Obligations**

Setu Credit Bureau
----------------------------------------
##### USAGE

* This module are use for creates a credit bureau report for the contacts.
* report are download from the menu **Credit Bureau Report** under the Contact --> Credit Bureau Report --> Create Credit Bureau Report.
* there are a **Bureau HD EM** and **Bureau AC CR DE AV** tabs are available in contacts and in those tabs there are a various fields are available by the groups like - **EM**, **AC**, **CR**, **DE**, which values are used in the report of credit bureau.
* there are a selection **Endorser** and **Shareholder** in contacts, if partner are not from MARVELSA so one of those are selected and if one of this are selected and Credit Bureau Report are are also checked then another new field are enabled to filled which is **Contact Related** and it is filled as which MARVELSA partner's under these partner are, these MARVELSA partner are select here, and under those partner there are the field **Shareholder Contact Related** and **Endorser Contact Related** in the tab Bureau AC CR DE AV at these fields those partner are set respectively.

##### Configuration

* There are the field at the contact - **Credit Bureau Report** if it is checked only those types of contacts are enable to creating a report.
